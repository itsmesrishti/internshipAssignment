Q: Can I use Ashwagandha Bounce on oily skin? A: Yes. Ashwagandha Bounce is a non-sticky moisturiser that is suitable to use on all skin types including sensitive skin.

Q: Can I use Ashwagandha Bounce day and night? A: Yes. You can use Ashwagandha Bounce during the day and at night as the last step in your skincare routine.

Q: Does Ashwagandha Bounce have SPF? A: No. Ashwagandha Bounce does not contain SPF. We recommend layering it with Patchouli Glow SPF 40 during the day for sun protection.

Q: Should I use Ashwagandha Bounce all year round or should I just use it in winter? A: You can use Ashwagandha Bounce twice a day, throughout the year.
